  2. Most of those questioned refused to participate, also/ as well as/ even/ too when offered money.
  3. Spainish is spoken in several South American countries, also/ as well as/ even/ too in Spain.
  4. Also / As well as / Even / Too being one of the leading experts in the field, she is also/ as well as/ even/ too is trained violinist.
  5. It seems as if no expense is too great in order to prolong life, also / as well as / even / too when patients clearly only have days or weeks to live.
  6. This is not only true of students, Professor, also/ as well as/ even/ too, will suffer if the government introduces these measures.
  7. Also / As well as / Even / Too if this were true, which it certainly is not, it would have no effect at all.
